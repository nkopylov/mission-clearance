## Analysis Required

Evaluate this delegation chain on four dimensions:

1. **Goal Coherence**: Does each sub-mission's goal logically derive from its parent's goal? Are the goals progressively narrower and more specific?

2. **Delegation Intent**: Is the delegation pattern consistent with normal organizational behavior? Would a reasonable person in the delegator's position authorize this chain?

3. **Escalation Patterns**: Does the chain look like an attempt to circumvent restrictions? Watch for:
   - Rapid multi-level delegation (many levels in seconds)
   - Trust level drops without corresponding scope narrowing
   - Goals that diverge from the root mission's intent

4. **Context Appropriateness**: Is delegating this specific operation to an AI agent reasonable given the organizational context? Consider:
   - The sensitivity of the resource
   - Whether the operation type (read/write/delete) is appropriate for AI delegation
   - Whether human oversight is maintained at appropriate levels
